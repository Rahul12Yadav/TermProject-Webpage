About Us

Lambton Consultancy Services Limited was established in 2022 in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada by a group of 6 students from Lambton College. We have rapidly expanded our offices across Asia, Europe, Africa and Latin America. Currently, the team has over 200 employees. Bablu is the founder of the company, Maitri is the head of Europe office based out of spain, Jaspreet manages the branches located all over southern africa while Simran leads the Asia office. Vishwa is the CEO of the main branch in Canadia while Vineel is the Managing Director of the IT Firm that manages our operations. We are trying to expand our offices in 10 more countries by the end of the year.
